Вариант 11

Household Chores

#11
🎧 Аудио к варианту: 11.mp3

1

What time do you usually come back from school?

I usually come back from school at about three or four o’clock. Sometimes I return later because I have extra classes. My school day is quite busy.

2

How much time does it take you to do your homework?

It usually takes me about two or three hours to do my homework. Maths and physics can take a lot of time. If we have many tasks, I finish my homework in the evening.

3

What are your Sundays like?

My Sundays are usually calm. I sleep longer, do my homework and spend time with my family. I also try to relax before a new school week.

4

What chores do you do at home to help your parents?

I help my parents with simple chores. I take out the rubbish, clean my room and sometimes wash the dishes. I think every teenager should help at home.

5

What chores would you like to delegate to a home robot?

I would like to delegate cleaning the floor and washing the dishes to a home robot. These chores are boring and take time. A robot could make everyday life easier.

6

What advice would you give to a teenager who hates cleaning his or her room?

I would advise him or her to clean the room little by little. It is easier to spend ten minutes every day than to clean everything at once. Also, a clean room helps you study better.

Вариант 12

Hometown

#12
🎧 Аудио к варианту: 12.mp3

1

Do you live in the country or in the city?

I live in the city. My hometown is Kolpino, which is a district of Saint Petersburg. It is not as big as the city centre, but it has everything people need.

2

What is typical winter weather in your hometown?

Typical winter weather in my hometown is cold and cloudy. It often snows or rains, and the streets can be slippery. Sometimes the weather is windy because we live near Saint Petersburg.

3

What place in your hometown do you like most of all?

I like the parks and quiet streets in Kolpino most of all. They are good places for walking with friends. I also like that Saint Petersburg is not very far away.

4

What environmental problems do people have in your hometown?

People in my hometown have some environmental problems, such as air pollution and rubbish. There is also a lot of transport, so the air is not always clean. I think people should take better care of nature.

5

Would you like to stay in your hometown when you finish school? Why, or why not?

I am not sure yet. I may stay in Saint Petersburg because there are many good universities there. But in the future I may move to another city if I find a good job.

6

What would you do for your hometown if you were very rich?

If I were very rich, I would build more sports grounds and modern study centres for teenagers. I would also improve parks and make the town cleaner. It would be great to create more interesting places for young people.

Вариант 13

School

#13
🎧 Аудио к варианту: 13.mp3

1

What transport do you use to get to your school?

I usually get to school by bus or on foot. It depends on the weather and how much time I have. I live in Kolpino, so the way to school is not too difficult.

2

What does your school building look like?

My school building is quite ordinary but comfortable. It has classrooms, a gym, a canteen and computer rooms. Some classrooms are modern and have interactive boards.

3

What sports facilities does your school offer to the students?

My school offers a gym and a sports ground. Students can play football, basketball and volleyball. We also have PE lessons and sometimes sports competitions.

4

What foreign languages can the students learn at your school?

Students at my school can learn English. Some students may also learn another language, for example German. English is the main foreign language for most of us.

5

What clothes do you have to wear to school?

I usually wear dark trousers, a shirt and a sweater or jacket. Our clothes should look neat and formal. I think it is normal for school.

6

What would you change in your school if you were the principal?

If I were the principal, I would make the timetable a little less stressful. I would also add more modern equipment for physics and computer science. Besides, I would make more comfortable places for students to relax between lessons.

Вариант 14

Environment

#14
🎧 Аудио к варианту: 14.mp3

1

What environmental problem do you think is the most serious for our planet?

I think climate change and pollution are the most serious environmental problems. They affect people, animals and nature. If we do not solve these problems, life on our planet will become worse.

2

Describe one environmental problem in your hometown.

One environmental problem in my hometown is rubbish in streets and parks. Some people do not throw litter into bins. It makes the town look dirty and harms nature.

3

Does your family sort the rubbish before throwing it away? Why, or why not?

My family does not always sort rubbish, but we try to do it when it is possible. Unfortunately, there are not enough convenient recycling bins everywhere. I think sorting rubbish should be easier for people.

4

What can students in your school do to help the environment?

Students can collect waste paper, save electricity and take part in clean-up days. They can also use less plastic. Even small actions can help the environment if many people do them.

5

Does a career as an ecologist attract you or not? Why?

A career as an ecologist does not attract me very much. I am more interested in maths, physics and IT. But I understand that ecologists do very important work.

6

What would you recommend teenagers do to make their hometown a better place to live in?

I would recommend teenagers not to litter and to take part in clean-up activities. They can also plant trees and help make parks cleaner. It is important to respect the place where you live.

Вариант 15

Money Issues

#15
🎧 Аудио к варианту: 15.mp3

1

How much free time a day do you have on average?

On average, I have about two or three hours of free time a day. I study at a physics and mathematics lyceum, so I have a lot of homework. On weekends I usually have more free time.

2

What do you enjoy doing in your free time?

I enjoy playing computer games, watching videos and walking with my friends. I also like listening to music. Sometimes I read or learn something about computers.

3

How do you get your pocket money?

I get my pocket money from my parents. They usually give me some money for snacks, transport or small things I need. Sometimes I save money for something more expensive.

4

What do you usually spend your pocket money on?

I usually spend my pocket money on snacks, transport and small things for school. Sometimes I buy something for my computer or phone. I try not to spend all my money at once.

5

Would you like to take a part-time job? Why?

Yes, I would like to take a part-time job in the future. It would help me earn my own money and become more independent. But now school is more important for me.

6

What part-time job would you recommend to a teenager who wants to earn some extra money during the summer holidays?

I would recommend working as a courier, a shop assistant or a camp helper. These jobs can be suitable for teenagers during summer. But the job should be safe and not too difficult.

Вариант 16

Pets

#16
🎧 Аудио к варианту: 16.mp3

1

Have you got a pet?

I have not got a pet now. I like animals, but a pet needs a lot of time and attention. Maybe I will have a cat or a dog in the future.

2

What pets are most popular in your region?

Cats and dogs are the most popular pets in my region. Many people also keep parrots, hamsters or fish. I think cats are especially popular because they are good for flats.

3

What pets do you think would be dangerous to keep in a flat? Why?

I think exotic animals, big dogs or poisonous snakes can be dangerous to keep in a flat. They may need special care and a lot of space. Also, they can be dangerous for other people.

4

Why are people so attached to their pets?

People are attached to their pets because pets become part of the family. They give people love and positive emotions. Pets can also help people feel less lonely.

5

Why do people sometimes abandon their pets?

People sometimes abandon their pets because they are not ready for responsibility. A pet needs food, care and time every day. I think it is very cruel to leave animals alone.

6

What pet would you recommend to a person who has never had pets? Explain why.

I would recommend a cat or a small hamster to a person who has never had pets. These animals are easier to take care of than some other pets. But a person should still be responsible and kind.

Вариант 17

The Internet

#17
🎧 Аудио к варианту: 17.mp3

1

What do you use the Internet for?

I use the Internet for studying, communication and entertainment. I watch videos, play games and look for information. I also use it to prepare for school lessons and exams.

2

How much time on average do you spend online a day?

On average, I spend about three or four hours online a day. Some of this time is for studying, and some is for entertainment. I try not to spend too much time online.

3

There are many online courses available for free. What online courses would you like to take?

I would like to take online courses in programming, maths or physics. These subjects are connected with my interests and future plans. Free courses are a good way to learn something new.

4

How can the Internet motivate people to learn foreign languages?

The Internet gives people many useful materials for learning languages. They can watch videos, listen to podcasts and talk to foreigners online. It makes learning more interesting and convenient.

5

Do you consider all the information on the Internet reliable or not? Why?

No, I do not consider all the information on the Internet reliable. Anyone can post false or incorrect information online. That is why we should check facts on different websites.

6

How would you recommend teenagers avoid becoming addicted to the Internet?

I would recommend setting time limits and spending more time offline. Teenagers should do sport, meet friends and have hobbies. It is important to use the Internet wisely, not all day long.

Books

What books do you prefer to read?

I prefer to read adventure books, science fiction and books about technology. I like stories with an interesting plot. I also sometimes read books that are useful for school.

My favourite writer is Jules Verne. I like his adventure and science fiction stories. His books are interesting and connected with travelling, inventions and discoveries.

I do not borrow books from the library very often. I usually read books online or use books that I have at home. But sometimes I go to the library for school tasks.

I prefer e-books because they are convenient. I can read them on my phone or tablet anywhere. But printed books are also nice because they are better for the eyes.
